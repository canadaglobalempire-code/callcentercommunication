# Call Center Communications - SEO & Lead Generation Strategy

## Executive Summary
**Primary Goal:** Drive qualified traffic and generate leads from businesses seeking call center outsourcing services.
**Secondary Goal:** Build authority through educational content that ranks for research-phase keywords.

1. **Informational Intent** - Researching outsourcing options (top of funnel)
2. **Commercial Investigation** - Comparing providers and solutions (middle of funnel)
3. **Transactional Intent** - Ready to choose/engage a service (bottom of funnel)

## PRIORITY IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4) 🔴
1. **Optimize existing service pages** for primary keywords:
   - /inbound (inbound call center services)
   - /outbound (outbound call center services)
   - /bpo (BPO services)
   - /automated (IVR, automated services)
   - /multilingual (multilingual call center)

2. **Create industry landing pages** for high-value verticals:
   - /healthcare (healthcare call center services)
   - /insurance (insurance call center)
   - /banking (banking call center services)
   - /ecommerce (ecommerce call center)
   - /technology (SaaS customer support)

3. **Homepage optimization** for core terms:
   - "call center outsourcing"
   - "call center brokerage"
   - "outsource call center"

### Phase 2: Content Expansion (Weeks 5-8) 🟡
1. **Blog content targeting long-tail keywords:**
   - "How much does call center outsourcing cost?"
   - "How to choose the right call center provider"
   - "Outsourcing vs in-house call center"
   - "Nearshore vs offshore call center"
   - Industry-specific guides

2. **Create comparison pages:**
   - Inbound vs outbound call centers
   - Onshore vs nearshore vs offshore
   - Top call center locations by industry

3. **Case study expansion:**
   - Turn existing case studies into SEO-optimized pages
   - Target industry + result keywords

### Phase 3: Advanced Targeting (Weeks 9-12) 🟢
1. **Location-based pages** (if targeting specific regions)
2. **Problem-solution pages** (pain point keywords)
3. **Service-specific deep dives** (24/7 support, HIPAA compliant, etc.)
4. **FAQ optimization** for question-based queries
